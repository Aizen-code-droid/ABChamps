HELP & SUPPORT

We're here to ensure that your learning journey is smooth, fun, and frustration-free! If you need assistance while using the app, explore the sections below:

1

Getting Started

  • Tap "Start Learning" to begin exploring the alphabet
  • Use the navigation arrows to move through letters A-Z
  • Tap the sound icon to hear the correct pronunciation of each letter
2

Common Issues & Solutions

App not opening or crashing?

  • Restart your device and try again
  • Ensure you're using the latest app version

Audio not working?

  • Check that your device's volume is up and not muted
  • Ensure microphone/speaker permissions are enabled

Game not loading?

  • Some games may require internet access
  • Check your connection and try again
3

Parent & Teacher Tools

  • Track learning progress in the "Progress" section
  • Customize learning options in the "Settings" menu (e.g., difficulty level, audio cues, theme)
4

Account & Purchases

  • If you've made a purchase but don't see your content, try restarting the app
  • Restore purchases from the Settings
  • Contact support with your receipt if the issue persists
5
